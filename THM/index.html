The Highway Men

The Highway Men (THM) are dedicated to restoring and expanding 6b6t’s Nether highways.

Founded on 12 September 2024 by mingailas, THM clears over 100,000 blocks of debris daily, extending highways toward the Nether world border.

Leading members include Ryazor, yung, FloppaDude, oorange, and others committed to long-term infrastructure work.

A key tool in their operations is KitBot1, a custom-coded bot developed and maintained by oorange, which supplies kits to builders on the highways.

THM’s efforts make travel across 6b6t faster, safer, and more accessible—benefiting both veterans and newcomers alike.

To contribute or earn kits by helping, join the official THM Discord.
